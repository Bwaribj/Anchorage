Who We Are


With over twelve (12) years’ experience in community and microﬁnance banking, Anchorage MFB was incorporated on the 18th day of July, 2005, licensed on 21 December, 2005 and commence full community banking operation on the 30th January, 2006.


At the introduction of microﬁnance banking in Nigeria by the Central Bank of Nigeria CBN, Anchorage MFB applied for status conversion from community to Microﬁnance Bank this was granted on 8th May, 2007 holding to the quantum of business transaction the bank is engaged in, the very prudent growth of the bank grounded on a solid corporate governance culture and the sound ﬁnancial sustainability upon which the bank managers and Board of Directors pilot the aﬀairs of the bank

.

Anchorage MFB join in main stream banking in 2015 with the introduction of veracious electronic/real-time banking services to meet her customer convenient banking needs. We provide retail banking, corporate banking, investment, group and individual banking needs. With an array of active customer base of over ﬁfteen thousand (15,000) across the length and breadth of Nigeria.
